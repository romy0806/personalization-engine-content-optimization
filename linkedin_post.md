I recently built a portfolio case study on using advanced analytics and machine learning to personalize digital content recommendations.

The business problem: organizations produce large volumes of content, but often struggle to determine which content should be promoted, personalized, refreshed, or retired.

The solution: an end-to-end personalization engine that combines behavioral data, content metadata, user segmentation, predictive scoring, and recommendation logic.

The project includes:
- Exploratory analysis of user engagement behavior
- User segmentation
- Content-based recommendation
- Hybrid recommendation strategy
- Offline evaluation using precision@k, recall@k, and NDCG@k
- A/B testing simulation
- Executive-ready recommendations

What made this project meaningful to me is that it connects technical depth with business decision-making. Recommendation systems are not just algorithms; they are strategic tools for improving engagement, personalization, and growth.

This framework can apply across industries:
- Pharma: HCP engagement and patient education
- Consulting: personalization strategy and digital transformation
- Tech: product analytics and recommendation systems
- Consumer products: consumer journey optimization

I am especially interested in roles where advanced analytics, data science, and business strategy intersect.
